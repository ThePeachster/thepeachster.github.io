Welcome to the site! My name is Jason Peacher-Ton. I am a student at the University of Washington currently studying Informatics with a Cybersecurity focus. I am an avid landscape photographer and Ultimate Frisbee player

My interest in photography first began as an interest in cinematography. I made various videos over the years but never found the time to improve in that area. My last major project was a video documenting my adventures in Thailand. While I was there, I also found myself experimenting with photography. Out of hundreds of pictures, I came away with a few that turned out fantastic. I had become hooked. It was around this time that I discovered Thomas Heaton's Youtube channel. As I watched more and more of his photography vlogs, I became inspired to get outside and take my photography to the next level. I booked a trip to Iceland for the sole purpose of improving my photography. As a result, I am pursuing photography as far as it will take me. Due to my college budget, I can only save enough money to take trips one to two times a year, so I'm using the gorgeous Pacific Northwest as my landscape photography playground.

I found myself gravitating towards landscapes after being forced on a few hikes by my more outdoorsy friends. Mother Earth will never stop amazing me with it's natural wonders and beauty. It only feels right to try and capture how I feel when looking at it. The excitement I feel when going out for a shoot is addicting.

Outside of photography, I play Ultimate Frisbee for the University of Washington Sundodgers, and am a hardcore Super Smash Bros. Melee player/spectator. If you'd like to talk to me about anything, just shoot me an email at jasonpeacherton@gmail.com
